About the Dataset

This dataset contains comprehensive stock data for the top 10 video game companies. It includes valuable insights for researchers, analysts, and enthusiasts.

Variable Definitions

  • Date: The date of the stock record.
  • Company: The name of the video game company.
  • Ticker Symbol: The stock's ticker symbol.
  • Currency: The currency of the stock prices.
  • Open: The opening price of the stock on the given day.
  • High: The highest price of the stock on the given day.
  • Low: The lowest price of the stock on the given day.
  • Close: The closing price of the stock on the given day.
  • Adj Close: The adjusted closing price after adjustments for splits, dividends, etc.
  • Volume: The trading volume of the stock on the given day.
